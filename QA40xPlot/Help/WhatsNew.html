Add a Residual waveform display to the Scope test

Per user requests, this adds the ability to show the residual time series (data - fundamental) to the plot. It will show in the "Get" list on the left where either channel can be shown or hidden and colors may be manually set. This waveform is (currently) based on the series data minus any spectral data within half an octave of the calculated fundamental.
